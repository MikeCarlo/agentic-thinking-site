## Welcome

Welcome to **Agentic Thinking**, a place to explore the fascinating and rapidly evolving world of AI agents and autonomous systems.

## What We Cover

This site is dedicated to deep dives into:

- **Agent architectures** — how to design agents that reason, plan, and act
- **Tool use** — enabling LLMs to interact with external systems
- **Multi-agent systems** — orchestrating multiple agents to solve complex tasks
- **Practical guides** — real-world implementation tips and patterns

## Why Agentic?

The term "agentic" refers to systems that exhibit agency — the capacity to act independently, make decisions, and pursue goals. As LLMs become more capable, the ability to build reliable, safe, and powerful agentic systems is becoming one of the most important skills in AI engineering.
