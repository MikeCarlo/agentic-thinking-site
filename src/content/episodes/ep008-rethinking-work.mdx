## Key Takeaways

- ▸ The 2017 "Attention Is All You Need" paper introduced the transformer architecture that enabled massive GPU parallelization and is the direct ancestor of all modern LLMs.
- ▸ Agentic AI has not eliminated jobs — it has expanded individual reach, letting developers take on larger and more ambitious projects than ever before.
- ▸ The real bottleneck has shifted: writing code is largely solved; the unsolved problem is the full product development lifecycle — ideation through production support.
- ▸ The MCP and skills marketplace explosion makes tool selection a genuine challenge; quality and usefulness vary enormously and finding trustworthy tools requires active curation.
- ▸ Routing tasks to cheaper, non-frontier models is a worthwhile investment — high-quality outputs are achievable at a fraction of the cost when prompt and spec quality are high.
- ▸ Agentation.com lets you annotate UI elements on a website and feed them directly to an agent as structured context, enabling agent-driven UI iteration loops.
